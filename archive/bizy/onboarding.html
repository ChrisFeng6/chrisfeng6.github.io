The Task

Bizy is a powerful collaboration platform. It can be confusing to users at first and the large amount of activities it can perform can be intimidating to new users. I was given the task to research and design an onboarding experience which introduces the important features of Bizy in a friendly way and guides new users through some important interactions, on mobile platform. The project later evolved into making an interactive experience demonstrating how a team can streamline the decision process with Bizy, while still serving as an onboarding tool.

The Solution

A simplified orchestrated interactive experience demonstrating how an example team reaches a decision on recruiting channels by going through a series of activities on Bizy's platform.

This prototype is coded with P5.js in Javascript. Try the interactive version here

  • The interaction is divided into 12 different stages, each one or two demonstrates a feature.

  • The experience is simplified so that the user only needs to tap on the screen once at the end of each stage to enter the next. The rest of the animation takes place automatically.

  • Some activities like voting and ranking are quite complex in the real app. Some interactions are omitted or simplified to make the audience focus on the fucntion while not overwhelmed by the complexity.

An Orchestrated Interaction

After exploring a number of different ideas and iterating on a few of them, our team narrowed down onto the following design: a series of carefully orchestrated interactions to demonstrate the key features of our app: facilitating conversations with prompts and facilitating decision making through activities like brainstorm, voting and ranking. The interaction is broken into a dozen stages where each one or two introduces one feature.
